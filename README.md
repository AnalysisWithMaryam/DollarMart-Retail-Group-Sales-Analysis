# 📊 DollarMart Sales-Analysis

### 📙 Project Overview
 DollarMart Retail Group is a growing supermrket chain that sells variety of products including groceries,   electronics, clothin, home appliances, and sports equipmetn across multiple regions
 
 As the company expanded management found it dificult to quickly understand overall sales performance, customer behaviour and prodcut demand using their raw transactional data.

 >To support better decision making, the management team requested for an interactive sales dashboard that could  >provide clear insights into revenue trends, top-performing products, customer segment and regional performance.
 
 >The final 📉 dashboard allows users to filter result by year and region while providing insight into revenue    >trends, customer contribution and product performance.

### Tools and Techniques
* Microsoft excel
* Pivot tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers for interactive filtering
* Data cleaning and transformation
* Data visualization
* KPI calculations

 ### Dataset Overview
  Order ID, Order Date, Product Name, Product Category, Region, Sales Revenue, Quantity Sold

 ### Data Cleaning Process
 * Checked for missing or incomplete values
 * Removed duplicate records
 * Standardized date formats
 * Created calculated columns for revenue related metrics
 * Ensured consistent category and region naming

 ### Dashboard KPIs
 * Total Unit Sold
 * Total Sales Revenue
 * Average Revenue per Customer
 * Average Revenue per Product

 ### Excel Dashboard
 The excel dashboard includes the following visuals:
 * Year-over-Year Revenue Comparison (2023 vs 2024)
 * Monthly Revenue Trends
 * Revenue Comparison (Weekends vs Weekdays)
 * Top 5 Revenue Generating Products
 * Revenue by Age Segment
 * Revenue Distribution by Gender
 * Regional Sales Performance

 ### Key Insight
  1. Vacuum Cleaner is the top-performing product, with 752 unit sold, generating approximately $433,638 in revenue.
  >This indicates strong demand for this product compared to other items.

  Sport category generated the highest overall revenue among all categories.
  >Followed by:
  * Home appliances
  * Groceries

  2. January consistently recorded highest revenue accross both years.
  
  Customers within 19-56 age range contributed the highest revenue with $6,019,978
  >This indicates
  * New year purchasing behaviour
  * Seasonal Promotions

 3. The Top 5 customers contributing the most revenue includes:
 > * James Adam ($116,129)
 > * Kimberly Cook ($96,583)
 > * Benjamin Stewart ($92,809)
 > * Kathleen Kelly ($88,769)
 > * Keven Gutierrez ($87,978)

4. West Region generated the highest revenue in 2024.
>Meanwhile the northern region consistently underperformed in 2023 & 2024 across most product categories,
>Indicating a potential market opportunity or operational challenge.

### Recommendations
1. Expand High-Performing Categories
>Increase inventory and promotions on Sports and Home appliances products so as to capitalize on their strong >revenue contribution.

2. Introduce loyalty programs or tergeted offers for the top 5 customers and other high-spending segments.

3. Improve North Region Sales
>Investigate factors affecting the North region's lower performace and introduce lacalized marketing
>strategies.

4. Focus marketing campaigns on customers ages 19-56, who represent the largest revenuer contributors.
